About

The longer version

I'm a product leader with experience building consumer-facing products at scale. I've spent the last 10 years focused on eCommerce but during that time have lived many tech lives — Web Designer, UX Designer, Product Manager, and now heading up a cross-functional Product group for URBN Inc. I care deeply about user experience, data-informed decisions, and shipping things that actually matter.

Lately, I've been leaning into AI — not just as a product area to watch, but as a toolkit for building. I use AI tools to code side projects, automate parts of my workflow, and stay close to what's technically possible. This site is one of those projects.
